# Test Flight Checklist
  * Appoint a designated checklist enforcer: 


## Itemized Summary
### Devices
  * Router (1)
  * NUC (1)
  * Blue RFD (1)
  * Ubiquiti Nanostation (1)
  * Ubiquiti Rocket AC (1)


### Power
  * Voltage Regulator (2) or Wall Outlet Adapters (2)
  * Batteries (5)
  * PoE Injectors (2)


### Cables
  * God Cable (1)
  * Ethernet Cables (6)
  * Turquoise Ethernet Cable for Camera (1)
  * Black Power Cable for Camera (1)
  * Barrel Connector to JST Cable for Jetson power (1)
  * iPhone cable for Wi-Fi hotspot (1)
  * USB-A to USB-C hub dongle (1)


## Setup
### Ground
#### Router
  * Router
  * Voltage Regulator #1 (set to 12V) with Battery #1 or Wall Outlet Adapter


#### NUC (broach)
  * NUC
  * Voltage Regulator #2 (set to 19V) with Battery #2 or Wall Outlet Adapter
  * Blue RFD to NUC USB port
  * Ethernet cable #3 from NUC to router "LAN" port


#### Nanostation
  * Ubiquiti Nanostation
  * PoE injector #1
  * Ethernet cable #1 from Nanostation to PoE Injector "PoE" port
  * Ethernet cable #2 from PoE injector #1 "LAN" port to Router "LAN" port
  * Battery #3 connected to XT60 on PoE injector #1


#### Antenna Tracker
  * Ethernet cable #4 from Arduino to router "LAN" port
  * Battery #4


### Plane
#### Jetson
  * Jetson Orin Nano
  * God cable (labeled "god")
  * Barrel connector to JST cable
  * barrel end goes in Jetson
  * JST end goes in god cable
  * Battery #5 to white end of god cable
  * Turquoise Ethernet Cable from Jetson to Camera


#### RocketAC
  * Rocket AC
  * Ethernet cable #5 to Poe port on Poe injector #2
  * Ethernet cable #6 from Poe injector #2 lan port to USB-Ethernet on Jetson


#### Camera
  * Camera
  * Black power cable from Camera to TX60 (yellow) connector on God cable


## Pre-Flight
  * Test image before mounting camera
  * GCS connection up
  * Router page open
  * Test image after mounting camera (remove lens cap!)
